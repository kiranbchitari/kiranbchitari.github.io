Privacy Policy for Kannada Abhyasa

Kannada Abhyasa app respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Kannada Abhyasa mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use Kannada Abhyasa:

  • Email address, when you create an account or sign in.
  • Learning progress, lesson completion status, quiz scores, and preferences.
  • Device and technical information such as device model, operating system version, app version, and crash diagnostics.
  • Advertising-related data such as advertising ID and ad interaction data, if ads are shown in the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To save and sync your learning progress.
  • To personalize your app experience.
  • To provide support and respond to your requests.
  • To improve app performance and fix crashes.
  • To show ads, if advertising is enabled.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Account Information

If you create an account in Kannada Abhyasa, your email address is used to identify your account, help you sign in, and restore your learning progress across devices. Google Play requires disclosure of this kind of personal information collection in your privacy policy and Data safety form.

4. Data Storage

Your account information and learning progress may be stored securely on our servers or through trusted third-party backend services used by the app. Some preferences may also be stored locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Kannada Abhyasa may use trusted third-party services to support app features. Depending on the app version, these may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob

These services may process limited personal or technical information as required to provide their functionality.

6. Advertising

If Kannada Abhyasa shows ads, ad providers such as Google AdMob may collect limited device and advertising information to serve and measure ads. This may include advertising identifiers and usage events.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only with service providers that help us operate the app, such as authentication, analytics, crash reporting, hosting, and advertising providers.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account information and learning data for as long as your account remains active, or as needed to provide the service. If you request deletion of your account, we will delete or anonymize your data unless we are required to keep some information for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons. Google Play expects developers to explain retention and deletion practices clearly.

9. Account and Data Deletion

Google Play requires that users be able to request account deletion from inside the app and also through a web-based method.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by:

  • Using the Delete Account option inside the app, if available.
  • Contacting us at the email address below.
  • Using our account deletion request form by emailing: kiranbchitari@gmail.com

After verification, we will process your deletion request within a reasonable time. Some limited data may be retained where required for legal or security reasons.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

11. Children’s Privacy

Kannada Abhyasa does not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate compliance measures. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page.
